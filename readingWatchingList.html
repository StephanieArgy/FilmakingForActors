Reading and Watching Suggestions

Filmmaking in General

  • Making Movies (Sidney Lumet)
  • On Film-making: An Introduction to the Craft of the Director (Alexander Mackendrick, Paul Cronin and Martin Scorsese)
  • Hitchcock/Truffaut (Alfred Hitchcock and Fran√ßois Truffaut)

Editing

  • In the Blink of an Eye: A Perspective on Film Editing (Walter Murch)
  • The Conversations: Walter Murch and the Art of Editing Film (Michael Ondaatje)
  • First Cut: Conversations with Film Editors and First Cut 2: More Conversations with Film Editors (Gabriella Oldham)
  • The Cutting Edge: The Magic of Movie Editing (DVD)

Cinematography

  • Masters of Light (Dennis Schaefer)
  • Visions of Light (DVD)
  • Cinematographer Style (DVD)

Sound and Sound Design

  • Dialogue Editing for Motion Pictures (John Purcell)

Visual Effects

  • The VES Handbook of Visual Effects: Industry Standard VFX Practices and Procedures (Susan Zwerman and Jeffrey A. Okun)
  • The Invisible Art (Mark Cotta Vaz and Craig Barron)
  • The Art and Science of Digital Compositing (Ron Brinkmann)

Life on Set

  • Acting in Film: An Actor's Take on Moviemaking (Michael Caine)

And, for a slightly alarming representation of the director/actor relationship, you could watch this 15-minute short film, called Scene, which we directed for Dabhand Films in Scotland. (Lead actor Bryan Larkin won a Scottish BAFTA for his performance in the movie.)
