Glossary

This is the start of an ongoing glossary of terms relating to filmmaking -- both on-set and off.

"Cowboy"
A shot that shows someone's body from the knees up.
Dolly grip
Grip who moves the rolling dolly, on which the camera is sometimes mounted.
Gaffer
Head of the lighting department; also "chief lighting technician," especially in England.
Key grip
Head of the grip department.
"Oner"
A shot done in one single, uninterrupted take: no cuts, no coverage, no editing.
"Over"
A shot of an actor in which which we see part of someone else's body in the foreground, usually their shoulder and part of their head.
Steadicam
A vest-mounted arm, used to support the camera for extremely smooth handheld shots. Named for a specific system, but often used generically for any similar camera-support apparatus.
Two-shot
A shot that shows two people.
